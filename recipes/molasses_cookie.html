Molasses Cookie

Description

These molasses cookies are soft and chewy. This is my mom's recipe and one of my favorites. They store well and can be frozen. Great for gifting during the holidays!

Molasses cookies are a delightful treat known for their rich, deep flavor and soft, chewy texture. They are typically made with ingredients like molasses, brown sugar, butter, and a blend of warm spices such as ginger, cinnamon, and cloves. These cookies often have a crackled sugar crust, adding a slight crunch to each bite.

These cookies are a nostalgic favorite for many, often enjoyed during the holiday season but perfect for any time of the year.
